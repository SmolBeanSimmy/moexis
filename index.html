Live Life Moexis

Moexis is a feeling, an action, a way of life

Definition:

Moexis:v: Creative excitement. A random burst of energy that makes you want to do something creative. ex: "wow! After days of doing nothing, I feel so Moexis! I'm going to paint a picture!" ajd: The act of doing something creative because of being recharged from doing nothing all day. ex: "After taking a 6 hour nap, I have such an Moexis urge to bake cookies"
